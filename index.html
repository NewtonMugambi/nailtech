FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

How long do gel/acrylic nails last?

With proper care, a full set lasts 3–4 weeks. We recommend fills every 2–3 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Will nails damage my natural nails?

Not when applied and removed correctly! Our technicians use gentle techniques and never over-file. A proper removal soak prevents peeling or thinning.

What's the difference between gel and acrylic?

Gel is more flexible and natural-looking with a glossy finish. Acrylic is harder and more durable — better for long extensions or heavy use.

How soon before my wedding/event should I book?

Book your full set 1–2 days before the event. Schedule a fill 1–2 days before if you already have extensions.
